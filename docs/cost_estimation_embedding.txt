     "ProductCategory": LabelDef(

     python scripts/sample_embedding_tokens.py

  In plain English:

  • To embed the entire Knowledge Graph with 1,000 recipes,
    1,000 products, 1,000 ingredients and 1,000 customers,
    the one-time cost is $0.0067.

  • Every month, as 1,000 customers log meals and generate plans,
    the ongoing embedding cost is $0.0087 per month ($0.1044 per year).

  • The TOTAL cost for the entire first year is $0.1111.

  • Relationships (edges in the graph) have NO embedding cost.
    Only nodes with text properties are sent to the embedding API.

  • Nodes that already have embeddings are NEVER re-billed.
    Only NEW nodes added in the future incur cost.

  ─── Context: what does this compare to? ─────────────────────────────────

  • A single ChatGPT-4o API call costs ~$0.005–$0.015.
  • Your entire Year 1 embedding bill ($0.1111) is less than
    the cost of 11 ChatGPT-4o calls.
  • Embedding is the cheapest AI operation in this system.
    The LLM calls (meal plan generation, AI chat) cost 100–500× more per call.
